How to Cook maggie

Ingredients:

  1. 1 packet Maggi noodles
  2. 1½ cups water
  3. Tastemaker sachet
  4. 1 tsp oil or butter

Step by step cooking process:

  1. Heat 1½ cups water in a pan.
  2. Add Maggi noodles.
  3. Add tastemaker.
  4. Cook for 2–3 minutes while stirring.
  5. When water thickens slightly, turn off the gas.
  6. Serve hot.
