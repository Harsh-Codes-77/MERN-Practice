Welcome to Our Library

City Library has been serving readers for many years. People visit here to read books, prepare for exams, attend workshops, and spend quiet time learning new things.

Our library contains books related to science, history, programming, business, novels, and many other categories. We also have newspapers and magazines available daily.

Reading Facilities

Students can use the reading hall from 8:00 AM to 8:00 PM. Free WiFi and charging points are available for members.

  • Silent Reading Area
  • Computer Section
  • Group Discussion Room
  • Reference Book Corner
  • Daily Newspapers
